Bone Fracture Detection

Can you detect fractures better than AI?

Why it matters

Problem

Fractures are common and require fast diagnosis. Errors can affect patient outcomes.

Solution

AI helps doctors analyze X-rays faster and more consistently.

Impact

Reduces workload and improves accessibility of medical diagnostics.

Dataset

We used the GRAZPEDWRI-DX dataset containing over 20,000 X-ray images annotated by medical experts with bounding boxes.

Preprocessing

Images were converted into YOLO format and enhanced using medical augmentations to improve model robustness.

  • Brightness & contrast adjustment
  • Noise injection
  • Rotation & flipping
  • CLAHE enhancement

Model 1: YOLOv8

Fast and accurate detection model with strong localization performance.

Model 2: YOLOv11

Improved recall and better detection of subtle fractures.

Results

The models achieved strong performance across key metrics:

  • mAP: ~0.71
  • Recall: up to 0.89
  • F1 Score: ~0.72
